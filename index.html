About NUSH Hack

NUSH Hack 2022 is an annual team-based hackathon which aims to provide a golden opportunity for young and blooming programmers in NUS High to put their coding skills to the test in a 24-hour hackathon, where they code out apps and program systems to compete with their peers, based on a central theme.

In groups of 2-3 students, you are to make a prototype application (ideally with a Graphical User Interface), based on a theme which will be announced right before the hackathon, in the form of an administrative briefing.

Teams will be allocated to a presentation and judging slot. Each presentation slot is 10 minutes, 7 minutes will be allocated for presentation, and 3 minutes for Q&A.
